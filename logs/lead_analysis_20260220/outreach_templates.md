## Follow-Up Strategy

### For all leads:
1. **First contact**: Use templates above
2. **Follow-up #1** (3 days later): "Just checking in - did you get my message about..."
3. **Follow-up #2** (1 week later): "I know you're busy. I'll make this simple - can I send you 3 examples of websites we've done for [similar business]?"
4. **Final follow-up** (2 weeks later): "Last time reaching out - if timing's not right, I understand. But your competitors ARE investing in websites right now. Let me know if you'd like to talk."

### Red Flag Handling:
- **"I'm too busy"**: "That's exactly WHY you need a website - to capture leads when you can't answer the phone."
- **"Websites are expensive"**: "Our starter package is [price] and most clients get ROI in the first month. Think of it as one extra job per month."
- **"I don't need a website"**: "How many customers do you think searched for [your service] today and called a competitor because they had a website?"
- **"I'll think about it"**: "Fair enough. Can I send you a quick case study showing what happened when [similar business] got their website?"
